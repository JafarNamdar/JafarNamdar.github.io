I am a Ph.D candidate in Business Analytics at at Tippie College of Business, the University of Iowa. I have the pleasure of being advised by Professor Jennifer Blackhurst and Professor Gautam Pant. My research involves application of econometrics, machine learning/AI, and operation research to derive insights about risks and disruptions in global supply chain networks.
